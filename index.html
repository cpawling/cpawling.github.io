  • Tell Me More

A little about me, I am an undergraduate student at the University of Maryland
studying Geographic Information Science and Information Science as a double major.

I fell in love with maps at a very young age and ever since have wanted to create maps that make a diffrence.
In college I discovered I also enjoy analyzing data and creating visualizations in order to show data in creative and easy to read ways
I also discovered my enjoyment of coding and programming in college that made me want to double major into
the field of Information Science to learn more about how we can use data and coding to create some magic.

My goal is to use my intrests to further my career goals and help people understand
the world through both maps and the data that makes up our world.

Coding Languages

I have experience in a variety of coding languages such as HTML and CSS. My primary languages though are Python and R.
both of these languages I have a variety of experience in including GIS Python programming and scripting as well being proficent in using a
variety of diffrent packages such as MatPlotLib, Shapely, and Pandas.
